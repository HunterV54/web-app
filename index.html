FSU Web-App

by Hunter Vaughan, Kenneth Sasser, Lance Hamic

Posted on July 13, 2018

A common problem at Florida State, and pretty much all colleges or universities across the country is in regards to textbooks. Buying them, selling them, and trying to find the best deals along the way. University bookstores consistently over charge for books whether you are buying or renting. Some offer a buy-back program but then they try and short change you. Many people sell their textbooks on online marketplaces such as craigslist of Facebook but it can be difficult to find exactly what you’re looking for with all the extra clutter.

This Mobile App will allow students at the same university to connect and create a diverse textbook exchange program. Textbooks could be organized by course code and by topics. This would allow students an easy and affordable way to buy, sell, or swap textbooks.
